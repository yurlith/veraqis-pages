Contact & support

During beta, support runs by email. No account, no ticket portal, no telemetry — just a direct line.

📨 Email us

support@veraqis.tech

One address during beta, for every kind of message below. No license is required during public beta.

What to include

  • What you were doing and what happened
  • The product (Android / desktop) and version
  • The file format, if relevant (never send sensitive data)
  • Your OS / device

Types of enquiry

Product support

Questions about installing or using the Android Archiver, or about a Recovery capability.

Beta feedback

Recovery-result reports, bug reports and suggestions from using the beta.

Security report

Disclose security issues privately. Until a dedicated security mailbox is set up, use support@veraqis.tech.

OEM / white-label

Discuss embedding the engine or future Fleet and white-label terms.
Please don't email us irreplaceable files. For a bug, a small non-sensitive sample or a description is enough to get started.
